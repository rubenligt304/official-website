Intro

Welcome! My name is Ruben, and I am a professional racing driver for Redeker Motorsports. I compete in the Rotax Max Challenge (RMC) Euro Trophy, specifically in the Rotax Max Senior class, which happens to be the most competitive class in the RMC.

I consider myself a highly talented racing driver with a dream to turn my passion into a profession. My ultimate goal is to compete against the world's best racing drivers and join the ranks of the most admired teams such as Porsche, Ferrari, and Mercedes. Despite the demanding nature of racing, I am fully committed to giving my all to achieve my dreams. Check out my awesome journey as I strive to make a mark in the thrilling world of motorsports!

Karting

Karting is often viewed as a distinct sport, setting itself apart from others, and some individuals may question whether it qualifies as a sport at all. Allow me to persuade you otherwise by shedding light on the physical demands involved.

Firstly, fitness plays a crucial role in karting. Contrary to the misconception that it might not require significant physical prowess, the reality is quite the opposite. Achieving optimal performance on the track demands unwavering focus throughout the entirety of the race. The level of concentration required is more demanding than one might initially assume.

Moreover, the physical condition of a karting athlete must be top-notch. Negotiating every corner involves experiencing forces of at least 3g. In instances where the track offers excellent grip, drivers can take corners at higher speeds, subjecting themselves to even greater forces. This heightened pressure necessitates a finely tuned and resilient body to withstand the challenges posed by the intense gravitational forces encountered on the track.

In essence, karting is a physically demanding sport that demands both mental acuity and a high level of fitness, debunking any notion that it lacks the athletic rigor associated with traditional sports.

Journey

I began my karting journey at the age of 13, initially participating in indoor rental karting sessions. As my passion for the sport grew, I joined a karting academy, still within the indoor setting. After about six months, I took the next step and started competing in club races alongside friends I had made during my time in the academy.

In May 2023, I had a transformative experience when I had the opportunity to test a Rotax Senior kart. The combination of speed, weight, and the exhilarating g-forces completely captivated me. It was at this point that I made the decision to join Redeker Motorsports, a racing team that aligns with my aspirations.

Now, as a part of Redeker Motorsports, I find myself on a promising trajectory towards becoming one of the top racing drivers in Europe. The journey has been incredible, and I'm eagerly embracing the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in pursuit of my dream.

support

Navigating the world of motorsports can indeed be financially challenging, with costs often proving to be a barrier for many talented individuals. There are generally two avenues to sustain a racing career: substantial personal wealth or securing sponsorships from supportive companies willing to provide both financial and moral backing.

As I continue to pursue my passion and talent in this demanding sport, I'm confronted with the financial constraints that threaten to impede my progress. Despite my dedication and skill, I find myself struggling to meet the budgetary demands essential for success in motorsports.

To any company willing to make a meaningful investment in my racing journey, I extend a heartfelt request for collaboration. Your financial and emotional support could make a significant difference in propelling me towards the pinnacle of this sport.
